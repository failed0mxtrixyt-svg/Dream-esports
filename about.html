ABOUT DREAM ESPORTS

Our journey, mission, and commitment to excellence

Our Story

Dream Esports (DE) is a professional esports organization founded in 2023 with a focus on Rainbow Six Siege. Our mission is to develop talented players and compete at the highest level while maintaining a strong community presence.

With two competitive rosters, we provide a pathway for emerging talent to grow and succeed in the professional scene. Our academy team serves as a training ground for future stars, while our main team competes in top-tier tournaments.
